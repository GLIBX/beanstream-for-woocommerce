Beanstream

Beanstream allows you to process credit cards through its gateway. This plugin aims to show anyone that they can use Beanstream to take credit card payments in their WooCommerce store without having to write a single line of code. All you have to do is copy Merchand id and API pass key to a settings page and you're done.

Get Credentials from Beanstream

You need to have an active gateway account with Beanstream.

  • Login to your Beanstream
  • In the top right corner of your account dashboard will be a “Welcome” message. Under this is a field labeled “Merchant ID” and Find 'API access passcode' inside the Order settings page
  • Copy these two values to your Woo Commerce Beanstream settings page and “Save Settings”
